Introduction


Ilerioluwa Oladimeji Aloba, known professionally as MohBad, was a Nigerian rapper, singer, and songwriter from Lagos. He was formerly signed to Naira Marley's Marlian Records and left the label in 2022. He was best known for his hit singles Ponmo, Feel Good, and KPK (Ko Por Ke) (You Will Always Be Remembered) with Rexxie which was nominated three times for The Headies awards 2022.[


Here's a time-line of Mohbad's life:


  • 1996 - Ilerioluwa Oladimeji Aloba popularly known as Mohbad was born on the 8th day of June, 1996 to a small family in Ketu Lagos, Nigeria.

  • 2019 - Mohbad stepped into the spotlight in 2019 after agreeing to a record deal with Marlian Records World.

  • 2020 - MohBad released his debut album, Light, in the fourth quarter of 2020 as a follow-up to his viral song Ponmo which featured Naira Marley and Lil Kesh.

  • 2023 - Mohbad's EP Blessed, his first after leaving Marlian Records World, was released in June 2023 under his own label, Imolenization.

  • 2023 - MohBad died after being allegedly treated for an infection on 12 September 2023, at the age of 27.
